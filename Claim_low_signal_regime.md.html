**Claim : too hard to work on a low signal regime** The *low signal regime* is when the expected number of signal S is very low compared to the expected number of background B (S << B). In this regime inference is very difficult because the effect of the nuisance parameters and statistical noise is hiding the effect of the parameter of interest. This document gather evidence that this regime is the major, if not the only, difficulty in the HiggsML dataset.
